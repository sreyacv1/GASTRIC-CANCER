# Journal Vetting Checklist — avoid predatory journals at low IF

At IF ~1.5–2.0 in oncology, most journals advertising that number are new,
non-indexed, or predatory. Before submitting to ANY journal, confirm ALL of 1–4.
If a journal passes 1–4, a low IF (1.7, 2.0) is perfectly fine.

## Hard requirements (must pass all)

1. **PubMed / MEDLINE indexed**
   - Go to https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/nlmcatalog/journals
   - Search the exact journal name → check "Currently indexed for MEDLINE" or PMC.
   - Not there → do not submit.

2. **Scopus indexed**
   - Check the Scopus source list: https://www.scopus.com/sources
   - Legitimate journals are usually in BOTH PubMed and Scopus.

3. **Impact factor is from Clarivate JCR (real), not a fake metric**
   - REAL: "Journal Citation Reports (Clarivate)", "JCR Impact Factor".
   - FAKE (ignore): "Global Impact Factor", "Universal Impact Factor (UIF)",
     "Cosmos IF", "General Impact Factor", "SJIF" — these are invented.
   - Verify at https://jcr.clarivate.com if you have access, or the journal's
     official page (not a third-party aggregator).

4. **Not on a predatory watchlist**
   - Beall's List (archived): https://beallslist.net
   - Cabells Predatory Reports (if institution has access)
   - For open-access: must be in DOAJ (https://doaj.org)

## Red flags (any one = walk away)

- Promises acceptance/publication in a few days.
- Solicits your paper by spam email ("Dear Eminent Researcher...").
- APC (fee) not disclosed until AFTER acceptance.
- Editorial board members with no verifiable institutional affiliation,
  or who don't know they're listed.
- Journal name nearly identical to a famous one (hijacked journal).
- Grammatical errors on the journal website; broken DOIs in past issues.
- No clear peer-review process described.

## Verified-legitimate options near IF 2 (oncology, as of 2026)

| Journal | ~IF | Indexing |
|---|---|---|
| World Journal of Surgical Oncology | ~2.0 | SCIE, PubMed, PMC |
| PeerJ | ~2.3 | PubMed, Scopus, PMC |
| Journal of Cancer (Ivyspring) | ~2.5 | PubMed, Scopus (verify current) |
| Cancer Medicine (Wiley) | ~2.9 | SCIE, PubMed, Scopus |
| Current Oncology (MDPI) | ~3.4 | SCIE, MEDLINE, Scopus |

Note: IF values change yearly (released each June by Clarivate). Re-check the
current number and indexing status at submission time — indexing is what
protects the publication, not the exact IF.
